Founded in 2019, Gargash Hospital is UAE’s first female-owned, multi-specialty, tertiary care hospital with primary focus on women and childcare. An expertise
center established by the flourishing success of our Founder , Dr. Husnia Gargash and her extensive medical experience and poles of excellence in the fields of
IVF, Gynecology, Obstetrics and General Health

Gargash Hospital focuses on holistic healing, embracing every breadth of human life — mind, body, and soul. It’s the first hospital to have a fully equipped
Assisted Reproductive Technique (ART) facility with modern amenities, advanced technology, and excellent health care professionals to promote a safe
environment and comfortable experience. Our ambiance ensures that patients feel relaxed as they visit the facility. The professional healthcare representatives are
trained to make every medical treatment personal and customized with luxurious comfort and care.

A major advantage of Gargash Hospital is our social commitment and strong understanding of bringing forward an affordable and service-oriented general care to
the patient. In Gargash Hospital, every patient will experience a compassionate medical team that cares about building families and maintaining their health to
overall wellness.

OUR GOALS

  • Provide effective state-of-the-art medical care to patients in an eco-friendly environment.
  • Educate health care professionals with exceptional knowledge, skills, and values.
  • Provide a premium private hospital experience for patients.
  • Develop affordable healthcare to all patients regardless of their social situations
  • Set a standard of excellence in the hospital industry .
