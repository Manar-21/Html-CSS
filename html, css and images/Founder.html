Gargash Hospital reflects the ultimate vision of its Founder and Chairperson, Dr. Husnia Gargash --- to
offer end-to-end solutions for all gynecological-related problems and fulfill a patient’s dream of having a
healthy family

Dr. Gargash is an Emirati born in Dubai, who obtained her post-graduate studies in Dublin and London.
After completing her education, she worked in the UAE for Dubai Health Authority, Dubai Medical College,
and as Department Head of Obstetrics & Gynecology at Dubai Hospital. Her global involvement in medical
communities combined with her reputable achievements over the years powerfully motivated her passion
to help enhance familial health care in the UAE.

Dr. Gargash is recognized as the first female Emirati gynecologist and IVF specialist in the UAE, who took
the lead on Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART) and Family Health. An established pioneering
woman with great compassion, genuine dedication, and strong determination, Dr. Gargash is also the first
Emirati consultant who opened her own Gynecology Centre,a first of its kind Assisted Reproductive
Technology (ART) facility, named Dr. Husnia Gargash Fertility, Gynecology and Obstetrics Centre
(DHGFGOC).

Since then, she continuously helps women and families with innovative solutions to solve their
reproductive health concerns. Building Gargash Hospital advances Dr. Gargash’s purpose and
commitment to providing medical expertise in a world-class patient-care facility, complying with the
premium quality standards of family-centered health care. Her dynamic personality and proactive
leadership will guide the hospital and devoted team of healthcare professionals, to deliver excellent quality
patient care that nurtures in a way, only a woman understands.
